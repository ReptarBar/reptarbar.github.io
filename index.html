Brief 00

Why communities stall

Invisible friction

Docs, templates, and labels drift away from reality, forcing newcomers to guess your real process.

Slow reciprocity

Contributors ship a fix but never hear back or get named, so they do not return for round two.

Safety debt

Moderation paths, escalation options, and expectations exist in people’s heads, not in writing.

Program drift

Community teams run hard, but metrics hide the places where trust and predictability are leaking.

How a confidential audit works

A three-step playbook that stays light on your team’s time while giving you precise fixes.

01

Recon & targeting

Define the surfaces that matter most (docs, repos, chat, onboarding, events). You approve scope and timeline.

02

Field run

Agents move through your ecosystem like a new contributor. We open issues or PRs when appropriate, logging screenshots and timing.

03

Dossier & handoff

You receive anchored scores, a prioritized backlog, and optional readout so your team can ship improvements immediately.
