Our Method, Unclassified

We work quietly, like the Robin Hoods of open source - not vigilantes, but provocateurs of progress. We look for patterns, not blame. We identify issues and ignite improvements, returning power to the people who build the web’s future.

Secret Shopper Mission

Enter a project like a first time contributor. Observe how it feels to join, communicate, and contribute. Report patterns, not people.

What you will do

  1. Pick one open source project or community.
  2. Try one small interaction, a question, a first issue, or running an example.
  3. Record your experience using the report template.

What you will rate, 1 to 5

  • Entry and Onboarding — clarity of getting started
  • Communication and Tone — responsiveness and respect
  • Documentation and Clarity — accuracy and completeness
  • Recognition and Follow Through — acknowledgement and motivation
  • Culture and Safety — inclusion and professionalism
  • Ecosystem and Sustainability — optional health checks
Ethics, in short
  • Do not reveal personal details or name individuals.
  • Describe experiences, avoid blame.
  • Submit with your assigned Agent ID.
